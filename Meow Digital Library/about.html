About the Meow Digital Library

Welcome to the Meow Digital Library, where we celebrate and educate about Felis Catus, commonly known as the domestic cat. Our digital platform houses a rich collection of resources, including text, images, audio, and video, highlighting the diverse species of cats worldwide. Each cat is categorized by country, showcasing their unique genetic diversity and special companionship qualities.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to enhance global awareness of cats through comprehensive digital resources that promote research, education, and responsible pet ownership. We aim to provide accessible and reliable information to benefit both cats and those who cherish them.

Objectives

  • Provide current and accurate information on cat species.
  • Support research and education with the latest studies and resources.
  • Promote understanding of the unique characteristics and genetic diversity of cats worldwide.
